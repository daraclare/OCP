Heating

Central Heating Systems and Solar Panels

Heating Systems installation and repairs

Radiators - upgrading/replacement to more efficient quicker heat up time radiators and fitting of thermostatic valves for individual temp control in each room.

Power Flushing Radiators – power-flushing of radiators, boilers and pipework for removal of sludge, dirt, debris for a more efficient and responsive heating system and a hotter and more evenly heated radiator.

Solar panels - installation and servicing of solar hot water panels/ tubes system for the free supply of hot water to your home.

Heating filters – fitting of MagnaClean filters for continuous year round cleansing of heating systems for protection of the boiler and its components and a more efficient system to run.
